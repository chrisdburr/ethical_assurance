What is Ethical Assurance?

Ethical assurance is a process of using structured forms of argumentation to provide assurance to affected individuals and stakeholders that a particular claim (or set of related claims) about an ethical property of a system is warranted given the available evidence.

The ML/AI Project Lifecycle

Ethical assurance has been designed specifically to enable a participatory approach to assuring the ML/AI project lifecycle in mind. This means that ethical principles, such as promoting fairness or social justice, ensuring explainability of automated decisions, and respecting user's data and privacy are tightly interwoven into the methodology.
